Kamal Haasan confirms reuniting with Rajinikanth on screen after 46 years

Indian cinema is set to witness history — the biggest news of the decade, about a pairing that could set all box office records on fire, is here. After weeks of speculation, legendary actor Kamal Haasan on Sunday (September 7) confirmed that he will reunite with superstar Rajinikanth on screen after 46 years in an upcoming film.
